Penguin SMP

Penguin SMP is an immersive and optimized lightweight medieval-themed server designed specifically for Minecraft 1.21.1 NeoForge. With a focus on performance and seamless gameplay, it provides a unique and captivating environment for players to explore, build, and interact within a rich medieval world, all while maintaining fast load times and low system requirements. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or new to the game, Geode58's Server offers a smooth and engaging experience for all players seeking to dive into the world of NeoForge.
