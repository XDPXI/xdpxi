XD's Servers Policy

By using XD's Servers, you automatically agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Server Deletion Policy

Servers will be frozen after 7 days of inactivity then deleted after 14 days of inactivity. Make sure to keep your server active to avoid automatic removal.

2. Data and Backups

We do not guarantee backups of your server data. Please ensure you have local copies of any important files.

3. Resource Allocation

Servers are allocated resources based on their usage and demand. Abuse of resources may lead to throttling or suspension.

4. Security Measures

Users must follow security best practices to ensure their servers remain secure. We are not responsible for compromised accounts or data breaches.

5. Network Usage

Excessive bandwidth usage that negatively impacts other users may result in restrictions or additional charges.

6. Server Downgrade

If your server's average player count remains between 1-2 players over a certain period, it will be downgraded to a lower-end server to optimize resource allocation.

7. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Users will be notified of major changes that may affect their services.
